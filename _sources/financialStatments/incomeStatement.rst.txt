Derived KPIs
============

.. admonition::
    Revenue (Financial Services)
   
    .. math::
        REV = NII + II

.. admonition::
    EBIT (Excluding Financial Services)

    .. math::
        EBIT = REV - TOE

.. admonition::
    EBIT Margin (Excluding Financial Services)
    
    .. math::
        EBIT\text{ }Margin = \frac{EBIT}{REV}

.. admonition::
    Netto Margin
    
    .. math::
        Netto\text{ }Margin = \frac{NINC}{REV}
