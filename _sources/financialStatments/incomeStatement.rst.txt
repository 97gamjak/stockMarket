Income Statement
****************

Basic KPIs
==========

.. list-table::
    :header-rows: 1
    :class: tight-table

    * - KPI
      - ID
      - Description
      - IBKR ID
    * - Revenue (Excluding Financial Services)
      - REV
      - Revenue is the total amount of income generated by the sale of goods or services related to the company's primary operations. It is also known as sales or turnover.
      - RTLR
    * - Gross Profit
      - GRP
      - Gross profit is the difference between revenue and the cost of making a product or providing a service, before deducting overheads, payroll, taxation, and interest payments.
      - SGRP
    * - Selling, General & Administrative Expenses
      - SellGenAdmExp
      - Selling, general and administrative expenses (SG&A) are a company's total direct and indirect selling expenses and all general and administrative expenses.
      - SSGA
    * - Total Operating Expenses
      - TotOpExp
      - Total operating expenses are the sum of all direct and indirect selling expenses and all general and administrative expenses.
      - ETOE
    * - Net Income
      - NetInc
      - Net income is the total amount of income generated by the sale of goods or services related to the company's primary operations. It is also known as sales or turnover.
      - NINC
    * - Interest Income
      - IntInc
      - Interest income is the amount of interest that has been earned during a specific time period.
      - SIIB
    * - Non-Interest Income
      - NonIntInc
      - Non-interest income is the portion of an institution's income that is derived from non-interest sources.
      - SNII

Derived KPIs
============

.. list-table::
    :header-rows: 1
    :class: tight-table
    
    * - KPI
      - ID
      - Description
      - Formula
    * - Revenue (Financial Services)
      - Rev
      - Revenue (Financial Services) is the total amount of income generated by the sale of goods or services related to the company's primary operations. It is also known as sales or turnover.
      - :math:`IntInc + NonIntInc`
    * - EBIT (Excluding Financial Services)
      - EBIT
      - EBIT (Excluding Financial Services) is the difference between revenue and the total operating expenses.
      - :math:`Rev - TotOpExp`
    * - EBIT Margin (Excluding Financial Services)
      - EBITMargin
      - EBIT Margin (Excluding Financial Services) is the ratio of EBIT (Excluding Financial Services) to Revenue (Excluding Financial Services).
      - :math:`\frac{EBIT}{Rev}` 
    * - Netto Margin
      - NettoMargin
      - Netto Margin is the ratio of Net Income to Revenue.
      - :math:`\frac{NetInc}{Rev}`
